About Us

Srinivasa Build India Private Limited (SBI Group) is a premium engineering and construction company delivering industrial, structural, and heavy lifting solutions.

Our Journey & Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize heavy infrastructure execution in India, SBI Group (Srinivasa Build India Private Limited) has built a stellar reputation over the last 15 years. Based in Ballari, Karnataka, we have grown from a localized contracting firm into a leading heavy construction and engineering provider servicing major industrial giants across the country.

Our expertise spans complex structural erection, precise mechanical fabrication, and high-capacity lifting operations. We take pride in our ability to manage challenging project conditions, delivering work with speed, unmatched engineering precision, and a zero-incident safety record.

People & Engineering Expertise

At the core of SBI Group's success is our highly trained workforce. We believe that state-of-the-art machinery is only as good as the minds operating it. Our team consists of qualified civil and mechanical engineers, certified crane operators, professional riggers, and dedicated safety officers.

Every project is preceded by rigorous design analysis, risk assessments, and lift plan designs to ensure flawless execution on site. From large-scale industrial warehouses to precise mechanical installations in power and steel plants, our team brings a wealth of specialized knowledge to ensure your project's success.

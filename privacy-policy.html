Privacy Policy

At SBI Group (Srinivasa Build India Private Limited), accessible from www.sbigroup.in, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by SBI Group and how we use it.

For a better experience while using our website, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, and query details. The information that we collect will be used to contact or identify you and to respond to your inquiries.
